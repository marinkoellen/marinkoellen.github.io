    • Hello I'm Ellen.

      I am an Applied Mathematican at BHP
      I enjoy researching and designing creative solutions using mathematics, statistics, machine learning and data visualisation to help address business challenges

    Recruited as a Mathematics & Statistics graduate in 2018, I am currently working in the Algorithms & Applications Foundations team in BHP’s Technology function in Perth, Western Australia. I also study web application development part time so I can use both web development and analytics to have more impact in my current role and am working towards creating accessible analytics tools so people can make better decisions with data! I am passionate about diversity and inclusion within the technology and mining sector and promoting women and girls' participation in STEM.

    Outside the office, you can find me spending a lot of my free time with my schnauzer Miffy, reading recipe books or cooking/baking/eating out/anything related to food.
